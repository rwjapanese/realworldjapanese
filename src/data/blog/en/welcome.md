## The A/B/C framework

Every phrase we teach comes in three versions:

- **A — casual** (close coworkers, chat)
- **B — neutral polite** (default business Japanese)
- **C — formal** (clients, first contact, escalations)

Pick the right register and you stop sounding rude by accident.
